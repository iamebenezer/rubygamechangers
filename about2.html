Table-tennis DATA MINING MAT


A table-tennis data mining MAT is a technology that collect data such as; points played, points won, points lost, number of serve, number of long serves, serve won, serve lost, position of tennis ball on the table, direction of ball e.t.c during a table tennis game. The mat sends these pieces of information to what we call the ruby table-tennis analytical app for analysis. Itâ€™s like a mat that is portable, allow proper touch of the tennis ball, easy to place and remove from the tennis table, easy to carry about. It can be used on any table-tennis table. This a new piece of technology entirely as it does not exist before (what actually existed from our research is a smart table-tennis table) which is not easily moved about and expensive but this proposed technology will be cheaper when the technology is finally actualised.

We observe low number of people involves in sports like(table tennis, Skating, and skipping) in our school and community.

Problem Research:


When we visited the table-tennis club, we observe that there is always confusion & argument on what the score is, who is serving the ball, weather the ball touches the tip of the table, the net or if the ball is on the right spot.

Most importantly lack of information about individual players(opponents) such as; Player performance level (past or recent).

Our Solution:


To the identified problems, we develop(proposed) a technology we called tennis data mining mat or smart mat.

Component of Mining Mat:


Smart replay camera

Sensor strip

Ruby game changer analytical APP

Features of the mining mat:


The mat has a sensor strips lying beneath the mat, this sensor strips provides accurate coordinate(position) of the ball on the tennis table. The strip serves as a maker that pin-point the position of the ball on the table.

Function of Mining APP:


The function of the mining mat and the smart camera is to collect data during the game and forward it to the Ruby tennis analytical APP.

Ruby Tennis Player Analytical App:


The Ruby Tennis Analytical App, is an online platform that allows collaborations of table tennis players all around the community and even the world at large to;

Collaborate together

Share tatics

Share strategy

Learn new skills

Analyse their individual performance

HOW IT WORKS

It works by accepting data (information) from the smart/ mining mat/camera and analyze players performance.

1. Keep Record

2. Shows Calender

3. Shows Live Scores

4. Serve as date-base for all information such as;

i. Replay videos

ii. Player profile

iii. Player performance
